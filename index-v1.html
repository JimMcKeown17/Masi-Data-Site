Early Childhood Education

(Ages 2-5)

Our ECD children are outperforming control groups by 400%.

  • With a focus on Story Telling we ensure our children hear thousands of stories.
  • We begin teaching children their letter sounds and Phonics, the building blocks of readers.
  • We teach children to develop the Listening Skills that are the bedrock of literacy.

Primary School

(Ages 5-11)

Our Primary School children outperformed control groups by 43% in 2023.

  • We continue with Reading Stories to children, striving to ensure all hear 1000 stories before age seven.
  • After mastering Phonics, we teach children to blend, decode, and begin to write words.
  • When they are ready, we begin having the children Read Beginner Stories themselves.

Programmatic

It's critical to identify which aspects of the programme are most, and least effective. Knowing this allows us to focus resources on investigating, improving, and iterating upon aspects of the programme that are not as effective.

  • We closely track progress across Specific Literacy Skills
  • We track progress of children in different Grades and/or Stages of reading.
  • We track Dosage and correlations to progress.
  • We constantly launch Iterations of our programme and track their effectivness relative to other versions.
Full Analysis

Schools

It's critical to identify how each site (schools & preschools) is performing. The team will divert resources, training, and interventions towards underperforming sites, while seeking to learn from and identify stars among the overperforming sites.

  • We closely track progress across performance of Each School
  • We divert manager/mentor attention to Lowest Performing sites to ensure we focus attention on where it's needed most.
  • We Learn From what's working at our best performing sites.
  • In general, we Expand interventions at sites with the best performance.

Sites of Work

Masinyusane works in the Eastern Cape of South Africa. The majority of our work is in the city of Gqeberha, though we have a variety of rural and semi-rural project sites. In total, we are operating in over 100 schools and preschools.
