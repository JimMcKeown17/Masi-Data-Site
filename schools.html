Site-Level

Data Analysis

It's critical to identify how each site (schools & preschools) is performing. The team will divert resources, training, and interventions towards underperforming sites, while seeking to learn from and identify stars among the overperforming sites.

  • We closely track progress across performance of Each School
  • We divert manager/mentor attention to Lowest Performing sites to ensure we focus attention on where it's needed most.
  • We Learn From what's working at our best performing sites.
  • In general, we Expand interventions at sites with the best performance.

Performance by Sites

Preschools (ECD Centres)

It's critical to track our most effective and least effective Preschools so our mentorship team knows where to focus. The ongoing goal is to identify the lowest performing centers, identify reasons for underperformance, take action to remedy the situation, and then repeat the cycle. By doing this, we raise the 'floor' of performance of the entire programme and ensure all children receive the assistance they deserve.
